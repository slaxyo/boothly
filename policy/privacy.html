Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1st, 2025

Boothly Events Co (“Boothly,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Info: Name, email, phone number, business name.
  • Event Data: Booth preferences, event registrations, and check-in activity.
  • Payment Details: Payment confirmations (not card numbers).
  • Technical Info: Browser type, IP address, device, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To register you for events and manage your participation.
  • To process payments and confirm your booth or sponsor tier.
  • To send you updates, reminders, and event-related notifications.
  • To improve the Boothly platform and user experience.

3. How We Share Information

  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • We may share basic info (like your business name) with event organizers or sponsors when relevant.
  • We may share info when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use basic cookies to remember your session and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request a copy of your data at any time.
  • You may ask us to correct or delete your data.
  • You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at the bottom of any email we send.

6. Data Security

We use encryption and other safeguards to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll post the revised version here with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at hello@boothly.co.
